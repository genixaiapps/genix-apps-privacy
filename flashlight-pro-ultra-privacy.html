Privacy Policy – FlashLight Pro Ultra

Last updated: November 2025

FlashLight Pro Ultra is a utility flashlight app developed by Genix Apps. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how our app works.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.
The app is fully offline and does not use servers or analytics.

Camera Permission (Used Only for Shake-to-Flash)

FlashLight Pro Ultra requires the Camera permission only to access the device’s flashlight (LED). This permission is needed exclusively for the Shake-to-Flash feature, which turns the flashlight ON/OFF when you shake your phone.

The app does NOT:

  • Take photos or record videos
  • Access your camera sensor
  • Capture or process any images

The permission is used strictly to toggle the flashlight via hardware control required by Android.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not target children under 13 and does not collect personal information.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
