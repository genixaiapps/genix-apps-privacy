Privacy Policy – FlashLight Pro Ultra

Last updated: March 2025

FlashLight Pro Ultra is a utility flashlight app developed by Genix Apps. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how our app works.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.
The app is fully offline and does not use servers or analytics.

Camera Permission (Flashlight Access Only)

FlashLight Pro Ultra requires the Camera permission only to access the device’s flashlight (LED). The app cannot take photos, record videos, or use your camera sensor in any way.

The camera permission is used strictly for:

  • Turning the flashlight ON/OFF
  • SOS emergency signal
  • Shake-to-flash feature
  • Flash strobe/speed modes

No camera images are ever captured or processed. All flashlight operations happen locally on your device.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not target children under 13 and does not collect personal information.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising frameworks.
